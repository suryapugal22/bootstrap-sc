Patients, Caregivers and the Public

Every health research activity targets the safety and well-being of patients and the public. Their participation is the key in getting new, safe and effective treatment options and to further study diseases/conditions for their better management

STEM council is taking steps to connect with patients, caregivers and the public to increase awareness of and education on ethical clinical trials, patient rights, and how they can further volunteer in research activities. We greatly value the contribution made by patients and the public and hence want to do the best research that is vital to the healthcare and social needs which benefit the country

Know about Clinical Trials

The recent pandemic (COVID-19) has taken the term ‘clinical trials’ to every household

Every patient or volunteer who decides to participate in a clinical trial and makes informed decisions based on the specific clinical trial information presented to them. Nevertheless, it can become a tiring experience and hence we have provided resources and tools that can be used by those interested to know more about clinical trials. This attempt is to enhance the trial experience and increase awareness
