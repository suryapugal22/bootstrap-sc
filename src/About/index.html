About Us

The STEM council has been established to create a systematic approach to healthcare and research in India. We are committed to support Ethics committee, Investigational Site, Sponsors and Vendors with knowledge, awareness, innovation, technology, and workforce to deliver research activities with improved quality

This platform serves as a non-profit organization to accelerate and promote global research to reach India. Post COVID-19 pandemic, the important lesson learned is that every stakeholder in a clinical trial needs to be connected through a common means which utilizes technology so that there is lesser need to travel the world in search of partners. The goal is to facilitate each entity with the required information and tools so that the common goal of patient safety and ethical research is met with increased efficiency

Mission

To connect people and research and serve as a common platform for healthcare industry, clinical research professionals, Sponsors/CROs and patients giving equal opportunity to contribute to each other achieving collective growth and innovation

Vision

Our Vision is to transform the research environment in India and attract global clinical trials thereby bringing safe and effective medicines, treatments, and solutions

Approach

Spreading awareness of ethical trials – At the core of clinical trials lies the safety and ethics of patients / volunteers taking part in the trial. With ever increasing disease burden, the need for research is at its peak. It is hence the time to be aware of conducting research activities ethically, safely and with highest quality. Bringing together research professionals, ethics committees, hospitals, research organizations and facilitating industry standard training programs, webinars, and self-learning tools are some of the means used in spreading awareness of ethical research. We specifically provide training and technology support for Ethics committees and sites in India to improve quality and efficiency

Equip and guide individuals, institutions, investigators, and innovators – There are a variety of ways in which we can assist individuals, institutions, and innovators from awareness to execution of complex research activities. Our aim to support individuals through learning tools and to make institutions research active

Engaging with patients, public and caregivers – No research is possible without voluntary participation of patients and/or public and the support provided by caregivers. It is important that they are involved and informed. We are taking that extra mile to bring clinical trials to patients and public by being a platform of holding clinical trial related information and in expressing interest in participation

Partnering with Industry Leaders, funders, and charities – Diverse partnership and collaborations are key to enrich the research ecosystem in India. We are the right platform to attract national and international research, encourage innovation partners, join hands with funders and charities all of whom are driven by a common goal – Benefit patients/public and boost contribution to the economy

Governance

We are governed by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare - India , DCGI, CDSCO and ICMR guidelines along with guidance from international and local regulatory and ethical standards (like ICH-GCP , Declaration of Helsinki , GCP-India )

Technology

Today’s research has the most suitable technology resources as their backbone to deliver safe, effective, ethical, and quality results. We come equipped with innovative technological advancements to help lift the standards of ethical research. We continue to develop innovative solutions to suit the needs of research. We also welcome ideas for the untapped areas

SAGE - translates to Submissions and Approvals Gateway for Ethics and it is a web-based portal developed to streamline and improve the efficiency of the submission process through the entire cycle of a clinical trial. This service is free to use for IRB/IEC in India

STAR - is an automated electronic feasibility management system re-engineered to optimize the study feasibility process

Request for Proposal (RFP) - serving as a marketplace for research, you can review, select, connect, and request quotes/proposals for your trials. Cut short the timelines in linking with the right partners, vendors and/or resources

STEM LMS - One of the mission of STEM is to deliver quality learning programs and educational tools related to clinical research
