Science, Technology and Ethics in Medicine

Connecting People and Researchers - With Safety and Ethics

Committee taking an Initiative to support Healthcare and Research through Innovation and Technology, Providing an Open platform for the Public, Healthcare, Professionals and Researchers enabling Research to flourish in India

Mission

Our Mission is to streamline Healthcare and research framework in India to attract clinical trials and bring safe and effective medicine and service to the Public and Patients. We aim to achieve our goal through

Spreading awareness of Ethical Trials Partnering with Industry Leaders, Funders and Charities Equip and Guide Individuals, Institutions and Innovators Engaging with Patients, Public and Caregivers

Governance

We are governed by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare - India, DCGI, CDSCO, and ICMR guidelines along with guidance from International and local regulatory and ethical standards

Technology

The face of Healthcare and Research is changing rapidly across the globe and every country is adopting newer technologies to simplify processes. And so are we
