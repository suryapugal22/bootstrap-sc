Science Technology and Ethics in Medicine

Committee taking an Initiative to support Healthcare and Research through Innovation and Technology, Providing an Open platform for the Public, Healthcare, Professionals and Researchers enabling Research to flourish in India

Mission

Our mission is to streamline healthcare and research framework in India to attract clinical trials and bring safe and effective medicines and services to the public and patients.

Governance

We are governed by the Ministry of Health and FamilyWelfare - India, DCGI, CDSCO and ICMR guidelines along with guidance from international and local regulatory and ethical standards

Technology

The face of Healthcare and Research is changing rapidly across the globe and every country is adapting newer technologies to simplify processes. And so are we
